Refund Policy

At SweetShope, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We take great care in preparing and delivering our products. However, due to the perishable nature of sweets, we follow a strict no-return policy. Refunds are issued only under specific conditions outlined below.

1. Eligibility for Refund

  • If the delivered item is incorrect or damaged during transit.
  • If the order is cancelled before dispatch.

2. Conditions

  • Refund requests must be raised within 48 hours of receiving the product.
  • Proof of damage or incorrect item (photo/video) must be provided via email.
  • Products must be unused and in original packaging.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, email us at support@sweetshope.in with your order ID and issue details. Our team will verify and process eligible refunds within 5-7 business days back to your original payment method.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after placing the order.
  • Incorrect delivery address provided by customer.
  • Delay caused by third-party delivery services.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us at support@sweetshope.in.
